APEX ORACLE — WINNING EDGE RULES
RULE 01 — ONLY TRADE KILL ZONES
London (07–09 UTC) and NY AM (12–15 UTC) sessions produce 80% of all profitable intraday moves. Never trade the dead zone (20:00–23:00 UTC).
RULE 02 — MINIMUM 3 CONFLUENCES
Never enter a trade with fewer than 3 confirmed confluences. The more layers of confluence, the higher probability. 5+ confluences = institutional-grade setup.
RULE 03 — HTF DEFINES BIAS
Always trade in the direction of the H4 and D1 trend. Use M15/H1 for entry refinement only. Counter-trend trades should be avoided until advanced.
RULE 04 — MINIMUM 1:2 R:R
Never take a trade with less than 1:2 risk-to-reward. A 50% win rate at 1:2 R:R is profitable. Target 1:3 to 1:5 for premium setups.
RULE 05 — MAX 2% RISK PER TRADE
Risk no more than 1–2% of account per trade. This protects your capital through losing streaks and allows compounding during winning runs.
RULE 06 — LIQUIDITY FIRST
Smart money always hunts liquidity before making a move. Look for stop hunts above swing highs (sell-side) or below swing lows (buy-side) before entering.
RULE 07 — NO TRADING PRE-NEWS
Close or avoid positions 15 minutes before any high-impact news (NFP, CPI, FOMC, rate decisions). News creates false signals and stops out valid setups.
RULE 08 — MOVE SL TO BREAKEVEN
Once price reaches 1:1 R:R, move stop loss to breakeven. This eliminates risk while leaving profit potential open. Protect your capital first.
RULE 09 — MAX 3 TRADES/DAY
Overtrading is the #1 account killer. Quality over quantity. If you've taken 2 losses, stop trading for the day. Protect your mental capital too.
RULE 10 — JOURNAL EVERY TRADE
The traders who journal consistently outperform those who don't by a wide margin. Track entries, exits, confluences, emotions, and results. Your journal is your edge.
